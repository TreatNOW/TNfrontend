Welcome

TreatNOW is the new way to find and book really good last minute, health, hair and beauty appointments.

Here's how it works

  1. Clients send a request to TreatNOW for a nearby appointment available NOW or very soon.
  2. Salons & therapists receive the request by text or email from TreatNOW and respond with an available time. (Web notifications coming soon)
  3. The client books the appointment and pays straight away.
  4. You get 82.5% of your usual fee. TreatNOW covers the Paypal fees.

Nice and simple. Great for TreatNOW clients, great for TreatNOW partners!

Free sign up, no contract, no time windows, no vouchers, no booking system, no discounts, just bookings.

Soon to be available in 20 languages to reach tourists & business travellers.

If you are a registered salon or therapist, we'll need your pricelist, your contact details for appointments and your paypal email so we can pay you.

Here's our checklist:

  • TreatNOW will need to see proof of your professional certificates / qualifications.
  • TreatNOW has a copy of your current pricelist. (You can amend this anytime)
  • Provide your Paypal email for receiving payment. (This will not be used for any other purpose)
  • Email or mobile number for receiving bookings requests.
  • You have received the terms and conditions. (Also available on the website)

Business name, name and title of person authorised to sign up to TreatNOW
